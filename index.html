About PDF2Speech

What is PDF2Speech?

PDF2Speech is an innovative tool designed to transform technical and academic PDFs into content that is perfectly optimized for Text-to-Speech (TTS) applications. Whether you're reading a programming book, technical manual, or academic paper, our AI-powered processing makes the audio experience smooth and natural.

Key Features

  • Code-to-Description: Converts code examples into natural language descriptions
  • Image Captioning: Transforms figure captions and image references into descriptive text
  • Smart Chunking: Intelligently splits large documents to respect API token limits
  • Batch Processing: Processes content sequentially and reassembles automatically
  • Privacy-First: All processing happens client-side with your own API key
  • Customizable: Fine-tune the output with custom system prompts
  • Mobile-Ready: Works on any device with a modern browser

How It Works

  1. PDF Extraction: Your PDF is analyzed and text content is extracted
  2. Content Chunking: Large documents are split into manageable chunks that respect API token limits
  3. AI Processing: Each chunk is sent to Google's Gemini API with your custom instructions
  4. Reassembly: Processed chunks are intelligently reassembled with proper formatting
  5. PDF Generation: The optimized content is converted back to PDF format
  6. Download: You receive a beautifully formatted, TTS-ready PDF

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (no frameworks)
  • PDF Processing: PDF.js library for text extraction
  • AI Engine: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash REST API
  • PDF Generation: jsPDF library
  • Storage: Browser localStorage for settings

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our top priority. PDF2Speech is designed with privacy-first principles:

  • üîí Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage
  • üîí No data is sent to our servers
  • üîí PDFs are processed entirely in your browser before being sent to Gemini
  • üîí You maintain complete control over your documents and API usage
  • üîí No tracking or analytics

Supported Models

  • Gemini 2.5 Flash: Recommended for most use cases. Fast, cost-effective, and capable.
  • Gemini 2.5 Pro: More capable but slower. Best for complex transformations.
  • Gemini 2.0 Flash: Stable and reliable. Good balance of speed and capability.
  • Gemini 2.5 Flash Lite: Lightweight model for simple transformations.

Best Practices

  • Start with Gemini 2.5 Flash for the best balance of speed and cost
  • Use lower temperature (0.5-0.8) for more consistent TTS output
  • Test with a small PDF section first to preview the output style
  • Adjust the system prompt to match your specific document type
  • Keep chunk sizes between 3000-5000 tokens for optimal results

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

PDF2Speech ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data and your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

PDF2Speech is a client-side application that operates entirely in your browser. We collect:

  • API Key (stored locally only): Your Google Gemini API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage and never transmitted to our servers
  • Settings: Your processing preferences (chunk size, temperature, model selection) are stored locally in your browser
  • PDFs: Your PDF files remain on your device and are not uploaded to our servers

3. Data Processing

When you use PDF2Speech:

  • PDF text is extracted in your browser
  • Content is sent directly to Google's Gemini API based on your configuration
  • We do not store, process, or have access to your PDFs or API responses
  • All processing occurs between your browser and Google's servers

4. Third-Party Services

PDF2Speech uses Google Gemini API to process content. When you use our service, your content is sent to Google's servers according to their terms of service. Please review Google's terms for more information about how they handle your data.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

PDF2Speech uses browser localStorage to save your settings and API key locally on your device. We do not use cookies for tracking or analytics.

6. Security

Your API key is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. We recommend:

  • Using HTTPS when accessing this application
  • Keeping your browser and operating system up to date
  • Never sharing your API key with others
  • Clearing your browser data if using a shared computer
  • Regularly rotating your Gemini API key

7. User Rights

Since we don't collect or store your data on our servers, you have complete control:

  • Access: Your data is visible in your browser's localStorage
  • Deletion: Click "Clear All Data" in Settings to delete everything
  • Portability: Export your settings at any time
  • No tracking: We don't track your usage or activity

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@pdf2speech.dev
